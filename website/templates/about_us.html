Who are we?


  As a provider of software solutions for tracking substance stocks in medical laboratories, we are proud to contribute to the smooth functioning of medical laboratories and clinics. We admire the effort put in by the medical professionals and are aware of the importance of a conducive work environment in the proper functioning of any medical laboratory or clinic.

 We believe that a promising work environment means transparency, organization, and efficiency. Therefore, our website is designed to help maintain these principles:

Alerts for expiration dates and minimum levels of medical substances
Our website constantly monitors medical substance stocks and can send notifications when a substance is approaching its expiration date or the minimum amount required for the cabinet to function. Thus, you can be sure that you will not have unpleasant surprises when it comes to medical substance stocks.

Consumption statistics
Our website provides detailed statistics on the consumption of medical substances in the last 3 months, 6 months or a year. This information can be useful for seeing clearly how you have spent resources and identify areas where savings could be made.

Consumption projections
Based on historical data, our website can make projections for future consumption. These projections can be useful in helping to plan and adequately stock medical substances, thus avoiding the loss of time and money with the additional purchase of unused stocks.


 Through the software solutions we offer, we sustain a conducive and efficient work environment for medical professionals, contributing to the development of a better medical system.
